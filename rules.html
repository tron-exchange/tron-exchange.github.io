Service Rules

1. Account Verification
All users must complete the account verification process to access the full range of services. This includes verifying your identity and providing necessary documents as per our KYC (Know Your Customer) policy.


2. Trading Restrictions
Users must adhere to the trading limits set for both deposit and withdrawal transactions. Trading activities that violate our trading policies, such as excessive high-frequency trading or price manipulation, are prohibited.


3. Security Measures
For the safety of all users, we require two-factor authentication (2FA) for account access and withdrawals. It is essential to keep your login details and private keys secure. Any unauthorized access to your account will be your responsibility.


4. Deposit and Withdrawal
Users must ensure that they provide accurate wallet addresses when making deposits or withdrawals. Tron Exchange is not responsible for lost funds due to incorrect wallet addresses.


5. Prohibited Activities
The following activities are strictly prohibited on our platform:

  • Engaging in illegal trading practices
  • Using the platform for money laundering or terrorist financing


6. Fees and Charges
Tron Exchange charges transaction fees based on the type of transaction, including deposits, withdrawals, and trades. All fees are clearly outlined in the user interface. Users are responsible for paying any applicable fees related to their activities.


7. Terms of Service Amendments
Tron Exchange reserves the right to update or amend these service rules as necessary. All users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of the platform will indicate acceptance of the updated terms.


8. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes or issues arising from the use of the platform will be resolved according to Tron Exchange’s dispute resolution policy, which may involve legal proceedings or mediation as deemed appropriate.


9. Service Availability
Tron Exchange makes every effort to ensure the availability of its platform. However, the platform may be temporarily unavailable due to maintenance, updates, or unforeseen circumstances. Tron Exchange will notify users in advance of scheduled downtimes.


10. Termination of Services
Tron Exchange reserves the right to suspend or terminate the accounts of users who violate these service rules or engage in unethical behavior. Users can request account termination in accordance with our policies.


By using the services of Tron Exchange, you agree to abide by these rules and terms. We value your cooperation and look forward to providing you with a secure and efficient platform for your cryptocurrency needs.
