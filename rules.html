Service Rules

1. Data Collection
TRON Exchange by GitHub only collects essential information during the exchange process. We temporarily store your email, TRX wallet address, and any other information provided by you to complete the transaction. Once the transaction is finalized, this data is securely deleted. We do not store any additional personal information or transactional data beyond what is required for the exchange.


2. Exchange Rates
The exchange rate provided on TRON Exchange is fixed at the time of your transaction. This ensures that you receive a stable rate, eliminating the risk of price fluctuations during the process. Please ensure you confirm the rate before proceeding with the exchange.


3. User Responsibility
It is the responsibility of each user to provide accurate information when initiating an exchange. Please double-check your TRX wallet address and other relevant details before completing the transaction. TRON Exchange is not liable for lost funds due to user error, such as incorrect wallet addresses.


4. Privacy and Security
We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of our users. All data you provide is encrypted and handled with the utmost care. We use advanced security protocols to ensure your information is protected during the exchange process.


5. Temporary Data Use
Any personal data you input, including email and wallet address, is only used for the duration of the exchange. Once the process is complete, this data is automatically purged from our system to ensure your privacy.


6. Service Availability
TRON Exchange strives to maintain 24/7 availability of its services. However, we reserve the right to perform scheduled maintenance or emergency updates as needed. During such times, users may experience temporary disruptions in service. We will notify users in advance of planned maintenance whenever possible.


7. Prohibited Activities
The following activities are prohibited on our platform:

  • Providing false or misleading information during the exchange process
  • Attempting to exploit or manipulate the system for fraudulent purposes
  • Engaging in illegal activities such as money laundering or funding unlawful operations


8. Amendments to Rules
TRON Exchange reserves the right to modify these service rules as necessary. Any significant changes will be communicated to our users, and your continued use of the platform will indicate acceptance of these updated terms.


9. Dispute Resolution
In the event of any disputes arising from your use of our platform, TRON Exchange will work to resolve the matter in accordance with our internal dispute resolution process. This may involve mediation or legal action if necessary.


10. Termination of Service
We reserve the right to terminate or suspend services for users who violate these rules. Any attempts to engage in prohibited activities or manipulate the platform will result in the termination of access to our services.


By using TRON Exchange, you agree to these service rules and commit to following them during your exchanges. We are dedicated to providing a secure and transparent platform for all users.
