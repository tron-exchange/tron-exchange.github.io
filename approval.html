Exchange Conditions

With Tron Exchange, you can exchange USDT to TRX at a fixed exchange rate! We do not charge any extra fees or interest for our exchange services. Transactions are processed quickly, usually taking about 5-15 minutes.


To make an exchange, follow these steps:


1. Fill in all fields of the submitted form.


2. Check the agreement for the provision of exchange services. If you accept it, tick the box and click the "Make an Exchange" button.


3. The exchange rate according to your application will be fixed after 1 confirmation on the network.


4. Funds are credited after 20 transaction confirmations.


5. Pay for the application by transferring the required amount to the address provided by our service after you enter the amount to exchange and the recipient's cryptocurrency wallet address. TRX will be transferred immediately after we receive your USDT.


6. After completing these steps, you will be redirected to the "Application Status" page, where the status of your transfer will be shown.
